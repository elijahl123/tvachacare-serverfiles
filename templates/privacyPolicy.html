Privacy Statement

Your Privacy is Our Policy

TvachaCare is a healthcare company that provides medical practitioners with a platform to manage their clinical data and to streamline their revenue cycle. Central to our mission is the commitment to be transparent about the data we collect, how it is used, and with whom it is shared.

For the purpose of this Privacy Policy, ‘personal data’ shall mean any information by which an individual may be personally identified, including name, address, e-mail address, telephone number, online identifiers, location data or any other information that is defined as ‘personal data’ under the California Consumer Privacy Act (“CCPA”) (“Personal Data”). 

This Privacy Policy applies to all visitors of the TvachaCare website (“You” or where the context provides “Your”) who’s Personal Data is collected or processed by TvachaCare. This Privacy Policy is intended to give you an opportunity to be informed about the ways in which TvachaCare collects, uses, and shares Your Personal Data. 

Categories of Data Processed

Data You Provide

During Your use of the TvachaCare website, You may provide and TvachaCare may process information regarding You and Your practice which may include Your name, scheduling information, email address, and telephone number. This information is only used by our team to contact You regarding the provision of TvachaCare Services. 

Cookies and Other Similar Technologies

We get information about Your IP address, proxy server, operating system, and web browser, visit time, device identifier, and features. We collect data through cookies and similar technology to identify and log Your use of the TvachaCare website. This information is used by TvachaCare to analyze and improve our security measures.

Other

We are constantly looking for ways to improve our website which may require the collection of new information and new ways to use Personal Data. If we collect materially different Personal Data or materially change how we use Your Personal Data, TvachaCare reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy accordingly and display it on our website. 

Mandatory Disclosures

TvachaCare may disclose Your Personal Data when we believe in good faith that disclosure is required by law or legal process, or if we have a good faith belief that disclosure is reasonably necessary to (1) investigate and prevent measures against suspected or actual illegal activities or to cooperate with public and government authorities; (2) enforce our contractual requirements; (3) protect and defend ourselves against any third-party claims or allegations; (4) maintain the security or integrity of the TvachaCare Services; or (5) safeguard the rights and safety of TvachaCare, its workforce and others.

TvachaCare will undertake good faith efforts to notify You about any legal demands for Your Personal Data when appropriate in our judgment, unless restricted by law or court order or when the request is an emergency. We may dispute such demands when we believe, in our discretion, that the requests are overboard, vague or lack proper authority, but we do not promise to challenge every demand.
